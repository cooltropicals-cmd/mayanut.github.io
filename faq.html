Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about maya nut, Brosimum alicastrum, and The MayaNut Company.

What is maya nut?

Maya nut is a common name for the tree species Brosimum alicastrum, a tropical rainforest tree in the mulberry family (Moraceae). Its seeds can be dried, roasted, and milled into a nutritious ingredient that can be used in beverages, baked goods, and more.

Where does maya nut naturally grow?

Brosimum alicastrum is native to tropical regions of Mesoamerica and the Caribbean. It is especially common in parts of southern Mexico and Central America and can also be found in some areas of northern South America and islands such as Cuba and Jamaica.

Where is The MayaNut Company located?

The MayaNut Company is based in South Florida, where the subtropical climate is well suited for growing Brosimum alicastrum. A dedicated plantation is being developed as the foundation for future maya nut beverages and related products.

When will your maya nut beverage be available?

Our goal is to begin offering a chilled maya nut beverage by around 2028, once enough trees have matured and we can reliably harvest and process seeds at scale. Until then, we are focused on planting, research, and product development.

Are your product images final?

Not yet. Most images displayed on our website are placeholders or concept designs that show the direction we hope to take with future products. As our plantation grows and recipes are finalized, we will update the images to match the real packaging and beverages.
