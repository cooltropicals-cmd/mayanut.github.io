In the Yucatán, many MayaNut trees are found around ancient Mayan sites — trees that the Maya likely planted centuries ago. The trees produce an edible seed, perfect for our future products. Our mission is to save Brosimum alicastrum from extinction by bringing it into modern sustainable agriculture.
