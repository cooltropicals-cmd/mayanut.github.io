The MayaNut Company was started in 2025 and is growing Brosimum alicastrum trees in South Florida. The MayaNut tree is an ancient tropical rainforest species, unknown to American agriculture. In the Yucatán, many MayaNut trees are found around ancient Mayan sites — trees that the Maya likely planted centuries ago. The trees produce an edible seed, perfect for our future products. Our mission is to save Brosimum alicastrum from extinction by bringing it into modern sustainable agriculture.
