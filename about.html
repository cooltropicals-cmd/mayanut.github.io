About MayaNut

MayaNut LLC is dedicated to cultivating the MayaNut tree, Brosimum alicastrum, in South Florida. August Kokus is the founder and current CEO of MayaNut LLC, working to introduce this perennial, climate-resilient crop to local agroforestry and regenerative agriculture projects. MayaNut™ (also known as Ramón, Breadnut, or Ojoche) is a nutrient-dense, caffeine-free seed used as a flour or in coffee-style brews, prized for its adaptability and benefits for both people and ecosystems.
