The MayaNut Company was started in 2025 and is growing Brosimum alicastrum trees in South Florida. The mayanut tree is an ancient tropical rainforest species, unknown to American agriculture. In the Yucutan, many mayanut trees can be found around ancient Mayan sites, which ancient Mayan peoples presumably planted for cultivation. The trees produce an edible seed, which are perfect for all of our future tasty products. Our goal is to save the species Brosimum alicastrum from extinction by utilizing it in agriculture.
