About

MayaNut LLC is dedicated to cultivating the MayaNut tree, Brosimum alicastrum, in South Florida. August Kokus is the founder and current CEO of MayaNut™.

Core Memory

Our mission: conserve Brosimum alicastrum, introduce it to South Florida agriculture, and build climate-smart, perennial food systems while protecting wild stands from deforestation and habitat loss.
